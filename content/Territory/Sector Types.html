Sector Types

Inner Sectors
...

Well developed sectors of space where most needs of every citizen are met. Technology is on high, up-to-date level and planets are full of metropolises. Like name suggests they are in the center of a faction's territory. Those regions are mostly peaceful due to high militarization.

Outlands
...

The edges of faction territory. Every faction has them. Planets on there are of class no higher than B, with most ranging in C or D. Wars often occur in the Outlands, as they are way easier targets than inner sectors. A lot of Graveworlds can be found in these sectors.

Uncharted Horizons
...

Planets here are either Rimworlds, Techworlds or unexplored. These sectors are usually highly contested, but also remain natural
