Corruption


There are cases where Alexis can become corrupted over time. It is extremely rare and their cases are very under documented, but it’s known that corruption is undiagnosable before the first symptoms show. Corruption can manifest itself as visual glitches of the visor, stuttering,  sparking, impeded movement, memory loss. If more than two of those symptoms occur simultaneously, it could be a sign of corruption, but sometimes those ‘glitches’ just happen.

At later stages of corruption, the disease is similar to dementia in organic beings, although corruption does not start in the brain, but instead in the BIC. Eventually, the Alexi will ‘forget’ either how to connect, breathe, eat, or their system becomes so corrupted that automated processes in the body fail (like regulating heart beat, CEE failure, AXR failure). If the head is forcibly put on life support, brain death will occur anyway after some time.

Corruption almost always leads to some form of psychosis, with the individual becoming delusional and experiencing hallucinations in the mid to late stages of the disease. The individual eventually becomes depressed, agitated, and/or aggressive. Corrupted Alexis will either shut themselves out and die in silence or go out with a burst of rage, destroying everything in sight, as the mere foundations of reality disappear in their minds.

Correctional firmware might help, but corruption is mostly a hardware issue in one of the chips that make up the BIC. Please note that correctional firmware will only delay the process and ease the symptoms, but corruption cannot be cured. Its progress may be pushed back, but in order to achieve this, the individual needs to take supplements regularly and/or have a low-stress environment.

Autopsy shows that in almost all cases the brain is not damaged in any way other than what would ultimately be the cause of death (most often oxygen deprivation). It is highly likely that corrupted Alexis are still fully aware (and most likely insane) while dying, and simply cannot do anything about it, being a prisoner of their own rotting mind.

The Intergalactic Federation is unsure what exactly causes it, although there is a positive linear correlation between Alexi rank and the rate of corruption. It is also found that Alexis who are exposed to huge amounts of stress can become corrupted more easily. One thing is known:

Once it starts, there is no cure.
