Before I start, these are NOT behind any paywalls, but are merely there to say what is expected, what is quirky, and also play a part in building your Alexi’s Model Identifier (which is fun!). There are three groups from most to least common:

  • Standard (A)
  • Uncommon (B)
  • Rare (X)

Rank
...

How you rank the entire Alexi is straightforward; take the least common two traits, and that is your Alexi’s rank, from left to right.

Examples:

  • all common - AA
  • 3 standard, 1 uncommon - BA
  • 2 standard, 2 uncommon - BB
  • 3 standard, 1 rare - XA
  • 1 standard, 1 uncommon, 1 rare - XB
  • 2 rare - XX

In the world, the higher the rankings of an Alexi, the more expensive to produce it gets, with an XX being reserved for the richest of owners, who can afford to pay up.

AA Alexis are mostly commercial, while XX are usually produced in military or industrial settings.

A list of uncategorized additional uncommon+ traits:
...

  • Artificial ears instead of biological (Uncommon)
  • Floating parts (i.e. not visibly connected; magnetism)
    • Small (ears, horns, tail) are uncommon
    • Big (limbs) are rare
  • Special abilities such as
    • Nanite wielding (simple, complex)
    • Telepathy (with other Alexis)
    • In general, if you think an ability is not that strong, it’s probably uncommon. If you think it is strong, it’s rare.
  • Integrated weapons are uncommon+

A list of disallowed traits (non-canon, goes against functionality):
...

  • No need to eat/drink/sleep (lack of organic needs)
  • Removable visor
  • Consciousness with a separated head
  • Multiple heads
  • Ability to reproduce (Genitals allowed)
  • Lack of internal organs
  • Liquid, gaseous, ethereal, or otherwise non-solid body (translucent is fine)
