Alexi - How Its Made

Alexis are grown in vats by The Intergalactic Federation using animal DNA over a 672 hour period (28 days). They’re grown with their full bodies (that is called a Pre-Alexi), which are later detached at the neck and processed separately in a similar manner. Pre-Alexis are dependent on the artificial conditions of the vats and do not experience consciousness nor pain until fully prepared. If one tries to remove a new, or even fully grown Pre-Alexi from a vat, they die within an hour. Some Alexis are created brain-dead, and those examples are used for replacement bodies only, because it’s way cheaper this way.

Once detached, the animal head is cooled to a near-freezing temperature and has its biological blood flushed out of its veins to be replaced by AX-Blood. The front half of the head is carefully removed with a clean machine laser cut and discarded. Then, a BIC is installed on the Alexi and a prefabricated visor is permanently attached to the head at the place of the cut. Simultaneously, a HBNC (head-body neck connector) is installed in the place of the neck cut. These two cannot be removed later without causing lethal damage. The Alexi head is now ready to be reheated to 37 degrees Celsius over a 4 hour period. After that, the head is VR trained over an 8 hour period in a state similar to sleep. When the process is done, the head is ready and can be activated. This is an Alexi.

The body undergoes a similar process, besides installing a HBNC and replacing the blood with AX-Blood, an AXR (AX Regeneration, responsible for replenishing AX-Blood) organ and a Calorie Electric Energy Converter are installed on the heart and stomach respectively.

Besides the compulsory, any and all additional modifications can be made. Even fully artificial bodies can be connected to an Alexi, but to list a few modifications:

  • Limbs that can be removed and replaced with Plug&Play connectors (which should be visible as a collar-esque ring, but can be obscured with fluff)
  • Jetpacks, roller blades, wings, and other quirky things can be installed (see Trait System)

After the entire assembly is completed, the Model Identifier is permanently engraved on the back of the HBNC at the rim.

Alexis are always created as adults, they experience no puberty, and have no sex (No XY/XX chromosomes). They are given a set of natural or artificial genitalia, can identify with a gender, and can have their body toned a certain way to fit a “male” or “female” standard, but otherwise, “male” and ”female” Alexis are identical.
