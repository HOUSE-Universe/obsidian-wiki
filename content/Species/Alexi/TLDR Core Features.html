TLDR Core Features

An Alexi, in its most basic form, is a head-shaped computer-assisted organic brain with a visor on it. For your OC to qualify as an Alexi it must:

  • Be partly artificial
  • Have a visor of any shape and size as its face (that can open, allow eating and breathing, see Visor)
  • Have a connector on the neck for a body (see HBNC)
  • Have an organic brain (aided by a Brain-Integrated Computer)
  • Have cyan (or close enough) blood (see AX-Blood)
  • Have functional internal organs (biological or artificial)

It must not:

  • Be an entire, inseparable body (without a neck connector)
  • Be entirely biological or entirely artificial (the brain must be organic)
  • Be able to function without anything connected to the neck slot for extended periods of time
